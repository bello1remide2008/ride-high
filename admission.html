Welcome to Admission Page 2025

At Ride High Edu Consult, we are committed to guiding students into the next phase of their academic excellence. This portal serves as your one-stop access to admission information, registration updates, and documentation for both new and returning students.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed admission form (online or offline)
  • Recent passport photographs
  • Valid means of identification (if applicable)
  • WAEC/NECO/JAMB result (for university admissions)
  • Proof of registration payment

Registration Process

1. Visit our main office for direct consultation
2. Submit all required documents.
3. Visit the main office for screening process and other registration
4. Receive your admission letter and proceed to course registration.
